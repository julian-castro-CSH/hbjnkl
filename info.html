What is a random Trivia?

Random trivia is interesting and cool facts about random things; it does not have to be about any particular subject, hence the name "random." The facts can range from general knowledge to complex and strange facts. In my trivia quiz, you will be given the option to pick the level of difficulty you would like to take. Easy being the easiest, and hard being the hardest question I have to offer.
